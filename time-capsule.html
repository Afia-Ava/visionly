Verify Your Account

To verify your profile so you can send a time capsule:

  1. When you try to submit a capsule, if your account is not verified, a modal will appear.
  2. In the modal, you have two options:
    • Verify Email: Click "Verify Email" to send a verification email to your inbox. Open your email and click the verification link, then refresh this page.
    • Complete Profile: Click "Complete Profile" to go to your profile page. There, set your display name (and any other required info), then return here and refresh the page.

Both your email must be verified and your profile (display name) must be set to send a time capsule.
